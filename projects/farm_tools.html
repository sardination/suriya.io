Farm Management Tools

The following tools are designed to simplify certain tasks involved in running a farm business and are currently in use to help run the CSA & retail operations at Amaranth Acres. If you are interested in obtaining any of these applications for your own farm, please contact Suriya at . Please include in your email whether there are any additional features you would be interested in seeing supported.

Worklog

Collect timesheet information for employees and volunteers on your farm. Individual workers can log in to clock in and clock out before and after their shift begins.

Features:

  • Supports multiple types of workers (workshare, volunteer, employee)
  • Assign wage rates to employees and determine payment owed over a time period
  • Manage all workers from a single interface

Ringup

Save products and prices to ring up customer purchases for retail sales.

Features:

  • Save product names and per-unit prices
  • Add unlisted products at ringup-time
  • Real-time ringup total calculation
